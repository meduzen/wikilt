Quick Overview

 We are very proud of our kiltmaking  and that we still use traditional methods and skills to make each kilt to the highest possible standard


through these pictures we shall try and explain the process that makes each kilt so individual and unique to the person for whom it is made


Each kilt is made exactly to fit the person who has purchased the kilt



  • - We guide you through the vast selection of tartans that are available from the best quality scottish mills

  • - A kilt come in 2 weights medium 13oz and heavyweight 16oz guidance is given as to which weight will be best for your use

  • - a minimum of 8 yards is used in each kilt

  • - The decision of pleating to the Sett or Stripe is then made

  • - Precise measurements are then taken by highly trained and experienced staff

  • - all our kilts are made with room for growth so you can buy with peace of mind that the kilt will last for years

  • - you are shown how the kilt should be worn


 


The kilt is then ready to be handmade



  • - the tartan is then pleated up to the exact measurements that were taken

  • - excess tartan in the back is cut away

  • - pleats on the kilt is handstitched 

  • -canvas stiffners are added

  •  

Product Description

The time honoured tradition of kiltmaking is still alive and well and in Demand,


With the move towards a throw away culture and the introduction of cheap imported goods we are still doing what we do best making kilts to traditional standards


our industry is becoming difficult to understand as some companies use the term hand finished implying the kilt is hand made when in fact it is not


The difference in hang and swing between a hand stitched kilt and that of a hand finished or machine made kilt is vast, because the kilt is individually shaped to fit each person, it fits snuggly in the small of the back and follows your shape to the widest part of the seat and then the pleats hang in a natural posistion and this gives the kilt the Hang and Swing that we have all come to associate with a kilt


If you are looking for the geniune article this is what we do best
